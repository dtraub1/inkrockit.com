InkRockit is an American owned company in partnership with vast print facilities in the People’s Republic of China, the same facilities that have been printing for many of the Fortune 500 companies for years. Today, through inkrockit’s unique partnerships, we can deliver your printing at a fraction of the cost of domestic sources. And, with China’s history of printing dating back almost 3,500 years, you can rest assured that the quality will be unsurpassed. As you can imagine, our standard turn-around time is affected by the great distances involved in printing overseas, but we believe you’ll find the money you save makes an up to six week delivery time more than tolerable. The bottom line is that we’ll save your company a great deal of money without having to compromise quality. At InkRockit, we oversee all phases of production to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Our Mission
Remove the barriers to printing overseas and bring the quality and savings home to every American business. Until now, only Fortune 500 companies have had access to the tremendous savings inherent in printing overseas; for everyone else, enormous obstacles have made it totally impractical. Inside these pages, you’ll find the lowest prices ever offered on the most popular printed items … the same items used everyday by individuals and businesses, to market, advertise and promote their products and services to the world .

We can also help you with those less popular projects as well. In fact, the more challenging your project is, the more money we’ll save you. And if you need high quality product packaging, custom boxes or point-of-purchase displays, InkRockit’s savings are out of this world.

One more thing … if you need something at warp speed, just let us know. In many cases, we can print and ship a partial of your order before it could even get off the ground here at home.  Or, if you need your whole order at light speed, our domestic pricing is quite competitive as well.
So when you’re ready to launch your next project, just call your sales representative, or visit us in cyberspace at www.inkrockit.com. We look forward to serving you in this new millennium.
